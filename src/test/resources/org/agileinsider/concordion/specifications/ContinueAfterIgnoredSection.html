Continue After Ignored Section

Example

When the Concordion Plus Extension is installed that adds the scenarioand ignore commands in the http://www.agileinsider.org/concordion/plus namespace. This command will ignore sections of the specification but should continue to process the remainder of the specification.

Running a specification containing: 

<div xmlns:cp="http://www.agileinsider.org/concordion/plus" xmlns:c="http://www.concordion.org/2007/concordion">
    <div cp:ignore="work in progress">
        <span c:execute="nonExistantMethod()">Should not try to execute.</span>
    </div>
    <div cp:scenario="Passing Scenario">Inside Scenario</div>
</div>

Should produce:

<div xmlns:cp="http://www.agileinsider.org/concordion/plus" xmlns:c="http://www.concordion.org/2007/concordion">
    <div cp:ignore="work in progress" class="skip">
        <span c:execute="nonExistantMethod()">Should not try to execute.</span>
    </div>
    <div cp:scenario="Passing Scenario" class="pass">Inside Scenario</div>
</div>
