This is a very simple scenario, which doesn't actually do anything but illustrates the markup applied.
Of course, some scenarios may fail due to Exceptions. And should be marked up accordingly.
But just because one scenario has an exception, should allow other scenarios to be tested and report success when they pass.
And of course, some scenarios may fail with a real failure caused by invalid assumptions, indicating a real failure in the implementation.
And of course, ignored sections should not execute any of the fixture code, which in this case would have resulted in a exception being thrown.
